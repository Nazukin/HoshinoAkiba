Artist Spotlight - Ado

  • Luisa
  • Date : August 26, 2022
  • Categories : J-Music
  • Tags : Ado ,Streaming

This week on artist spotlight we will bring the hottest artist that blow up the entire internet and wakes up a lot of japanese label to touch on potential of global streaming market because of just one song

Before the pandemic hits, Ado is already having a singing career as an utaite singer since 2017, releasing vocaloid cover such as Bad Dance Hall, Love Trial, Otome Kaibou etc on japanese video platform NicoNico or NND

However, things are getting wild for her career when she released her first original song called Useewa on October 23th, 2020 which by the way was one day before her birthday. With her rebellious voice and the lyrics that spark controversy among japanese people because it was very, very opposite of a japanese tennager manner, this song explode and as of right now it reaches 239 billion viewers on YouTube and reaches #1 on Spotify Japan Viral 50,Oricon Digital Singles Chart, Oricon Streaming Chart, and Billboard Japan Top 100

After the big success of Usseewa, Ado releases another song two months later with Readymade, 2021 saw nothing but more popularity for her as she releases 6 more singles including the most popular singles that year, Odo

Even with the success of her singles, Ado still makes cover song including Sheena Ringo's Tsumi to Batsu(Crime And Punishment), PinnochioP's Kamipoina(God-ish), and as usual one of unofficial anthem song for vocaloid community, Senbonzakura

Recently, Ado is chosen to provide her singing voice for Uta, newest character in One Piece Film RED which when as far as releasing entire album composing her and some of the top composer japan have to offer, such as Mrs Green Apple, Sawano Hiroyuki, FAKE TYPE
