Persona 4 Arena Ultimax for PS4, PC – rollback netcode update now available

  • Alex Mateo
  • Date : August 5, 2022
  • Categories : Games
  • Tags : Fighting ,Atlus

Publisher ATLUS and developer Arc System Works have released the rollback netcode update for the PlayStation 4 and PC (Steam) versions of Persona 4 Arena Ultimax.

Rollback netcode helps make online matches as smooth as possible, regardless of the other player’s connection.

For the PC version of the update, players need to opt into a beta branch. To do this, from Persona 4 Arena Ultimax in your Steam library, right click and go to “Properties.” Then, navigate to the “BETAS” tab and enter the beta access code: p4auopenbeta03. From the drop down menu, select “open_beta_3.”
